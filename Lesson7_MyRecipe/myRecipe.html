Grilled Cheese!

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 2 slices of sourdough bread
  • 2 slices of cheese of choice (I use Cheddar)
  • 1 teaspoon canola oil

Instructions

Preheat pan until hot.

Use a knife to spread butter on one side of both slices of bread.

place one slice buttered-side-down on the pan.

Place both cheese slices on the bread, and place te other slice of bread on top, buttered-side-up.

Flip the sandwich when golden brown, and do the same for the other side.

Place it on your favorite dish and let it cool for 5 minutes.

Cut if desired.
